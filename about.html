Our Story

InteriorHuts was born from a vision to redefine luxury living through exceptional design. Founded in 2008, we started with a simple belief: every space has the potential to become extraordinary.

Our journey began in a small studio in Mumbai, where Anil Sharma, fresh from his architectural studies, dreamed of creating spaces that would stand the test of time. Today, we've grown into a premier design firm with projects spanning across major cities in India.

What sets us apart is our commitment to understanding each client's unique story and translating it into spaces that reflect their personality while maintaining our signature minimalist luxury aesthetic.
